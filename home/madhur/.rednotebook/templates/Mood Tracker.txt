How are you feeling?

====================

- Numb
	Do Meditation , exercise etc
- Anxious
	Write down what is causing anxiousness. Do meditation to uncover.
- Motivated and Driven
	Write down what are you driven about
- Procrastinated
	Which task is causing procrastination? Try to find out reasons
- Happy
	What triggered happiness?
- Said
	What triggered sadness?
